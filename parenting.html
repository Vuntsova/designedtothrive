Parenting by Design

Understand your child's energy

Your child is not difficult. They may simply be designed differently than you. Human Design gives you a map for their needs, emotions, and natural rhythm.

Parenting a Manifestor child

They need to feel free and informed, not controlled. Give them room to initiate and ask them to tell you their plans rather than demanding permission.

Parenting a Generator child

They thrive on doing what lights them up. Let them respond rather than forcing choices, and protect their energy from obligation overload.

Parenting a Manifesting Generator child

Fast, multi-passionate, and prone to pivoting. Let them try and drop things without shame, it is how they find their real yes.

Parenting a Projector child

Sensitive, perceptive, and easily drained. They need recognition, rest, and invitation, not constant performance and busyness.

Parenting a Reflector child

Deeply shaped by their environment. The room, the people, the school, all of it matters more for them than for any other type.
