Business by Design

Build work around your actual energy

Stop copying someone else's business model. Your type shapes how you are built to create offers, make content, sell, and sustain, without burning out.

Content by type

The way you are designed to show up online differs by type. Forcing a daily posting machine when you are built for bursts is a fast track to resentment.

Offers by type

Some types thrive on high-touch premium work, others on scalable products. Your design hints at which offer shape will actually feel good to deliver.

Work rhythm by type

Sprint and rest, steady daily output, or response-driven, your sustainable rhythm is not the one in the productivity books.

Selling style by type

You do not have to sell the way the gurus sell. There is a way that fits your energy and does not feel like wearing someone else's clothes.

Burnout patterns by type

Each type burns out in a recognizable way. Knowing yours means you can catch it early instead of collapsing.
