The five types

The five Human Design types

Your type is the foundation of your design. It shapes how you are built to engage, decide, and spend energy. Find yours below.

01

Manifestor

You are here to initiate, impact, and move. You do not wait, you act, and you carry an energy others can feel before you say a word.

The common struggle: Learning to inform the people affected by your moves, instead of asking permission or going silent.

Best first step: Notice one place you have been asking permission you do not actually need.

02

Generator

You are here to respond to what genuinely lights you up. Your energy is sustainable when it is spent on the right things and draining when it is not.

The common struggle: Saying yes out of obligation to things your body already quietly rejected.

Best first step: Before the next yes, check whether your energy actually rises or sinks.

03

Manifesting Generator

You are here to move fast, pivot, and follow energy down several paths at once. Speed and multiplicity are features, not flaws.

The common struggle: Forcing yourself into one straight, linear path because that is what you were told success looks like.

Best first step: Give yourself permission to drop the thing that has gone flat.

04

Projector

You are here to guide, see, and direct energy. You read people and systems deeply and your gift lands best when it is invited.

The common struggle: Proving your worth through overwork instead of waiting for genuine recognition and invitation.

Best first step: Rest before you are empty, not after, and watch what gets invited in.

05

Reflector

You are here to mirror the health of your environment. You are rare, sensitive, and deeply affected by the spaces and people around you.

The common struggle: Forgetting that your environment is not a detail, it is the whole thing.

Best first step: Audit one space or relationship that is quietly draining you.
