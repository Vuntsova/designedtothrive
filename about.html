About

Emiliya Vuntsova

Human Design • Motherhood • Reinvention. Author of The Manifestor Mom and founder of Designed to Thrive.

I started over more times than I can cleanly explain. I tried every version of the advice, the routines, the productivity systems, the business models everyone swore by, and I kept ending up exhausted and wondering what was wrong with me.

Human Design was the first thing that did not try to fix me. It explained me. It gave language to the way I actually work, decide, and spend energy, and it made the patterns I had treated as failures finally make sense.

Designed to Thrive exists for the woman in that same place: the mother, the builder, the one who feels like too much or not built like everyone else. This is practical, honest Human Design, no woo, no guru act, just a clearer map for living and working the way you are actually designed.
